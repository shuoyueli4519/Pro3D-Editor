SVG-Eidtor: Single View Guide 3D Gaussian Edit with precise shape control

Yang Zheng1, Nan Chen1,
1University of Science and Technology of China
Paper Supplementary Code arXiv
In this work, we introduce SVG-Editor, a novel pipeline designed specifically for 3D Gaussian Splatting editing. It leverages any edited view as a control condition to perform accurate region editing on the original object.

Abstract

We achieve the local editing of object representations, which is based on 3D Gaussian Splatting. We have achieved precise region control editing, preserving the detailed features of the original object. The followings are 360-degree videos of 3D objects at an elevation of 0.

Comparison

The following presents a comparison of the results from our method with those of LGM、Tailor3D、MVEdit and 3DAdapter. Both our method and LGM generate objects are represented in 3DGS, while Tailor3D、MVEdit and 3DAdapter produce objects are represented in mesh. Besides, The results obtained from 3DAdapter and MVEdit are derived using their respective 3D-to-3D functionalities. Below are 360-degree orbit videos rendered at an elevation of 0.
